A few things are changing in the Broken Links HQ. The plan to record and release a 2-track single in December has now been scrapped, therefore the 18th December gig at The Joiners, Southampton has now been cancelled. Therefore our last gig in Southampton this year will be on 28th November at The Brook. We won't be back in Southampton until May/June time next year, when we will be releasing our first full-length album, containing all new tracks!!! Keep your eyes peeled on our progress on Facebook! There will possibly be some last minute secret gigs, showcasing the new material before it's release! For those of you in Germany, we have two Berlin gigs coming up at K17 and White Trash. It's going to be carnage, be there! We have also made it to the next round of the The Source's TV voting competition, our video for 'What Are You Waiting For?' will be shown on SKY TV on 24th October. More details to come, but please do vote for us. The winners of the competition get £10,000 and a record deal, which will help us complete the album. Stay safe.
