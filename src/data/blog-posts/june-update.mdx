We've just got back from a successful show at the Karel Music expo 2011, Cagliari, Italy....It was great to meet some new Italian fans, and hopefully we'll be back soon! Filming has now finished for our new music video for 'We're All Paranoid'! It's now in the editing stages and should hopefully be released in the coming weeks :) For our local UK fans, we're playing The Railway Inn, Winchester THIS SATURDAY (18th June), it's going to be a mammoth one hour set, with some other great bands playing, including our friends Get The Tape....it's £5 on the door...
