[Broken Links](http://www.brokenlinksmusic.co.uk "Broken Links Home") first self-release 'Resisting Movement And The Almost Advisory' will be officially available to purchase for download on Monday 30th March 2009. The EP will consist of four tracks: Within Isolation The Best Laid Schemes Of Mice And Men Colditz The Sea Inside The artwork has been decided upon and is shown below. CD's will be produced during March so will hopefully be available to coincide with the digital release of the EP. Check back for an update.
