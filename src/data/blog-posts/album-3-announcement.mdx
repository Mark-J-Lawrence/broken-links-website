We're pleased to announce that album #3 is on its way!

'Conflict::States' is an angry 12-track declaration aimed at the current state of the world. 

"_People are living in a world where they think they have choices. There are no choices. We’re on borrowed time. This album is a statement of awareness to all the shit holding society back._” - Mark

The album is due to land on 30th April 2021.

"_The 'difficult' 3rd album is complete! It's 100% BLT (Broken Links Tailor-made), and we were the tailors, but at the beginning, we didn't have the skills necessary to prepare this death-electro sandwich ![🥪](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/images/emoji.php/v9/tfc/1/16/1f96a.png)![🥪](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/images/emoji.php/v9/tfc/1/16/1f96a.png)_ 

_This record has been 100% recorded, produced, mixed, and mastered by us..and we've had to learn how to do that to a decent standard as part of this process.....its been painful, but hopefully you'll be blissfully unaware, thinking its had Quincy Jones' fingers all over it and a Sheen that's usually saved only for Hot Shots....This goes someway to explaining why its taken so long to make. The Brucie bonus in all this is that it'll take no time at all to record & release our next bunch of tracks ![😉](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/images/emoji.php/v9/t57/1/16/1f609.png)_ _You are not going to get anymore Broken Links than this record._

_Thank you for your patience Link'ers.....this thing will be in your ears soon_"

'Pioneers' will be the first single to drop from the album on 26th February 2021, along with a music video.
