_“If you’ve heard Broken Links’ debut EP ‘Resisting Movement And The Almost Advisory’ that was released earlier this year, then you already know what to expect from their second effort. Crunching industrial guitars? Check. Heavy bass? Check. A sound that flirts with stadium rock but ends up sleeping with dark and dirty rock? Check. Even if that above checklist appeals to you, be warned that this six-track EP lasts a whopping 41 minutes meaning it may test the casual listeners but for those willing to go along for the ride, every minute is glorious."_
