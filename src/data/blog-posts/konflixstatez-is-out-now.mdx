The wait is over, **Konflix::Statez** is out now, available to download **FREE** on [our Bandcamp page](https://brokenlinksmusic.us2.list-manage.com/track/click?u=b2ed6c2e33f82dc5e84b241ae&id=415526c5c6&e=3a782347bc) and to stream on all good streaming services ([Spotify link](https://open.spotify.com/album/0gnEO0kJ6JvyS9RRZwVpRV?si=07ykELi9RPyLcFPLqz8XXw), [Apple Music link](https://music.apple.com/gb/album/konflix-statez/1613610685))

_“Who likes banger's?? We do!…go and check out our new 7-track remix album of songs taken from our last album Conflict::States!! It's a free download on our bandcamp page, so download it, stick it on a USB, and pass it to the DJ at the next 40th Birthday party you go to!! Have a time Link'ers!...  
There was still life left in the last album...I mean there was gonna be, there's at least 7.752 billion people in the world that haven't heard it yet because they can't be arsed or simply can't, and that's fair enough. But for those of you that did listen, this is an opportunity to hear the songs again from a totally different angle!  
There's a massive mix of style's on this album so there should be something to suit everybody...but if you're looking for lounge music...shave off.  
A hugely massive thanks to all the remixers, they're the heroes of this album so go check out their other music from the links below!"_
