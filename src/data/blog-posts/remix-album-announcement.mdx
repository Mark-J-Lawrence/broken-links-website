10 months ago we released CONFLICT::STATES, containing some of the truest words we've ever written, which only seem to get truer and truer the more warped this world gets...

To celebrate the album, on Friday 25th March 2022 we will be releasing KONFLIX::STATEZ, a 7-track album of remixes of songs from the original!!

Massive shout out to all the remixers on the album, which quite aptly, all live in different countries across the globe, from Canada to Azerbaijan! They have taken our tracks and turned them into something completely different. Keep your eyes peeled for upcoming videos!!
